About me

Hello! My name is Miha Vidakovič and I live in Kranj, Slovenia. I do freelance web and graphic design, lift weights, eat ice cream and burgers, and explore the outdoors.

I specialize in creating websites that are user-friendly and built according to best-practice web standards. I also create corporate identity, branding, and other things. Over the past years, I've enjoyed working with wide array of clients which were pleased with my work.

Design is my driving passion. Everything in my life is affected by it, life decisions are based upon it. Understanding how great design works - and showing others the importance of well thought out, beautiful design.

My process

1. I sit down, open a Squared Moleskine and begin creating wireframing out the designs, giving solid thought to position, size and proportions. The next step is dig into Illustrator to finalise the wireframes to ensure a great user experience.

2. It’s Photoshop time! Taking the carefully crafted wireframes & userflows into consideration, I start to flesh out the user interface until I’m happy with it. Then it’s the time for little tweaks until the client is as well.

3. Here’s the really fun part. Taking those designs and using my frontend skills to make them awesome. Whether it be a quick static site, a content-managed site, or even an e-commerce store - it’s going to work extremely well.
